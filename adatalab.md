### R packages

- [hanwoo](https://github.com/adatalab/hanwoo) for modeling the nutrient requirement of Hanwoo cattle.   
- [dairyCattle](https://github.com/adatalab/dairyCattle) for modeling the nutrient requirement of dairy cattle.
